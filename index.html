Student2Student cleaning services connects Students seeking part time jobs to students seeking help with a few apartment chores.

Student 2 Student is a cleaning service that works exclusively on college campuses providing services to students through other students.
This is a social business designed to meet the needs of the university community internally,
building upon a mutual trust between students and accommodating the irregular availability of both clients and service providers.
